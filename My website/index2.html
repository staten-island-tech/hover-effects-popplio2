Our Music

Musical Doodle is a alternate-cyber-Martian-virtual-quantum-proton kind of band with classical roots and heavy Martian influence. What this basically means is that when we rock, we rock the floor, the house, the solar system, the galaxy, the universe, maybe even the multiverse. During one of our shows, we even created a very miniscule black hole, just from one of Jessica's amazing guitar solos.
